About registro.in

Registro.in is a website that act as a medium between people and the governrment. In this website people can pay thier utility bills, can get news about government projects, complaining about problems in their soicety. This website acts as a direct medium to solve any problem of the public.

How the idea poped out?

One fine morning I had utility bills that I had to pay to the government and it was the last date to pay the bills. I went to m near government office to pay the bill I got frausted because there were so many people in the que that day. But still I managed to pay bill that I had to pay.

After paying the bill I returned home and I was too tired. At that a idea poped in my brain. And the idea was basically to design a website where public can pay their utility bill through online.

Design

When it comes to designing the website so many poped into my head . But I want to design a website which can be easily understanble to everyone and is easy to navigate. So at first I design some wireframes. I have given different sections and given differnet categories so users don't find any problem while using the website.
